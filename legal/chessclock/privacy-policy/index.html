Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how ChessClock handles information when you use the app.

Last updated: March 7, 2026

This document is provided in English. If translated versions are made available, the English version shall prevail in case of any discrepancy.

1. Overview

ChessClock is a simple chess timer application designed for over-the-board chess games. The app is free to use, works entirely on your device, and does not require an internet connection.

2. Information We Collect

ChessClock does not collect, store, or transmit personal information.

  • The app does not collect personal data.
  • The app does not track users.
  • The app does not use analytics services.
  • The app does not display advertisements.
  • The app does not require login or account creation.

3. Internet Access

ChessClock does not require an internet connection to function. The app does not communicate with external servers as part of normal use.

4. Local Storage

Any settings or preferences you choose in the app may be stored locally on your device only so the app can remember your configuration. This information is not sent anywhere.

5. Children's Privacy

ChessClock does not collect personal information from users of any age. Because the app does not collect data, it is intended for a general audience, including children.

6. Third-Party Services

ChessClock does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

7. International Availability

ChessClock may be made available globally and may support multiple languages. Regardless of language availability, the app's privacy practices remain the same: ChessClock does not collect personal information.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If changes are made, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised.
